# **Packbreaker Arena — Concept Brief**

## **North star**

A browser-first, async inventory auto-battler where the bag is the puzzle and the round is the question. Every session is a 12–20 minute negotiation between greed (scale economy) and pragmatism (counter the meta). You log in, you build, you watch your bag fight a ghost, you adjust. No one is waiting on you.

## **Audience**

* **Primary**: 16–34, plays roguelites and autobattlers (Slay the Spire, Super Auto Pets, Backpack Battles, Balatro). Wants systemic depth in short bursts. Reads patch notes for fun.  
* **Secondary**: Lapsed strategy players who want "smart but not punishing." Time-poor, will play 2–4 runs in a sitting if onboarding is clean.  
* **Creator-adjacent**: Async \+ replays \+ daily seeds make builds shareable. Designed to be stream-watchable from day one.

## **USP**

The chill async cadence of Super Auto Pets meets the tactile spatial puzzle of Backpack Battles, in the browser, with daily contracts and ghost matchmaking that respect your schedule. Mastery comes from item adjacency and recipe trees — not from twitch, not from grind.

## **Pillars**

1. **Readable in one screen.** Bag, shop, opponent intent, and round state visible without scrolling. Desktop 1280×720 baseline, mobile 390-wide vertical.  
2. **Mastery from synergy, not fidelity.** Item interactions and bag arrangement carry the depth. Art clarifies; it never substitutes.  
3. **Async respects player time.** Ghost battles, daily contracts, replay log. No queues. No "log in or lose your streak" mechanics.  
4. **Cosmetic-only ranked economy.** Skins, board themes, emotes, frames. No paid power. Rewarded video confined to unranked.

## **Anti-goals**

* Real-time PvP at MVP.  
* Pay-to-win in any queue.  
* Sprawling open-world or persistent meta.  
* 3D rendering or expensive shaders.  
* Final art before systems are validated.  
* Custom asset pipelines before content systems prove out.

## **Success metrics (M2 demo gate)**

* **D1 retention ≥ 35%**, D7 ≥ 15%, D30 ≥ 6%.  
* **Run completion rate ≥ 55%** — runs started → resolved (won, lost-out, or boss-fought), not abandoned mid-shop.  
* **Median session ≥ 1.6 runs.**  
* **Time to first "I get it"** (proxy: first won round) **≤ 4 minutes** for new accounts.  
* **Item pick rate spread**: no item \< 2% in valid contexts, none \> 35%. A healthy spread is the proxy for synergy depth.  
* **Replay share rate ≥ 8%** of completed runs.

## **Why this works in browser**

Deterministic combat → thin server. Data-driven content → fast iteration. Drag/drop bag → native to mouse and touch. Daily contracts → retention without push spam. Ghost battles → social pressure without realtime infra.

## **Origin**

Selected from an 8-concept evaluation weighted desirability 35% / browser fit 25% / Claude-tool leverage 20% / retention 20%. Ranked \#1. Comparable proof points: Slay the Spire, Super Auto Pets, Backpack Battles, Backpack Hero, Die in the Dungeon. Full rationale: source PDF in project root.
